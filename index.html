How to Use ImgMaster Tools

  • Image Size Converter: Upload your image using the "Select File" option, enter your target size in KB, and click "Convert Image".
  • Quality Adjuster: Upload your image, adjust the quality with the slider, and click "Apply Quality Adjustment".
  • Image Format Converter: Upload your image, select the desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP), and click "Convert Format".
  • Image Filter Tool: Upload your image, choose a filter effect (e.g., Grayscale, Sepia, or Invert), and click "Apply Filter" to see the transformation.

Each tool provides clear status messages and progress indicators during processing.
